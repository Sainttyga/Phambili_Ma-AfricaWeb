Meet the Owner & Founder

Nkosinathi Ngema

13th November 2019 • phambilimaafrica

Phambili Ma-Africa Trading Enterprise is the result of vision, creativity, and the passion of young entrepreneurs. Established in 2007, we have built a strong reputation for quality and efficiency, consistently delivering beyond our customers' expectations.

Welcome to Phambili Ma-Africa! We believe in contributing to the growth of a more equitable South African economy. That’s why we encourage you to consider supporting a township business like ours.

We’re not just any business; we’re a product of the township—born and raised in the heart of it all. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to our work. We’re driven by a hunger to build and expand, yet we remain small enough to offer a personal touch to everything we do.

We are determined to offer innovative solutions to the cleanliness challenges we face every day. When you choose Phambili Ma-Africa, you're not just choosing a cleaning service; you're investing in a vision for a more inclusive economy.

Join us in this journey towards progress. Choose a company that stands for something more. Welcome to Phambili Ma-Africa—where community, commitment, and quality come together.
